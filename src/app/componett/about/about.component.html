ABOUT US

At MILLION HEALTH CARE, we constantly work towards ensuring access to high quality and affordable medicines to support patients in need. Which is why, we have been trusted by health care professionals and patients across geographies from decades.
As an organisation has been built brick-by-brick on the foundation of care. Caring For Life has always been and continues to remain, our guiding purpose. Driven by the same purpose, we have extended our presence in providing over 50 products across various therapeutic categories in 50+ dosage forms. To make healthcare more affordable globally, we are deepening our presence in the key markets of India, South Africa, the U.S as a RAW MATERIAL EXPORTER among other economies of the emerging world.
For over a decade, making a difference to patients has inspired every aspect of MILLION HEALTH CARE work. Our paradigm-changing offer of a NEUROLOGY CARE AND CONSULTANT PORTFOLIO acknowledged as having contributed to bringing inclusiveness, accessibility and affordability to the centre of range of products. A responsible corporate citizen, MILLION HEALTH CARE humanitarian approach in pursuit of its purpose of Caring for Life and deep-rooted community links wherever it is present, make it a partner of choice for global health bodies and stakeholders
